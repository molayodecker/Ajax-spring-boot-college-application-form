2. Field Technician (Environmental Health)
3. Field Technician (Community Health)

5. Field Technician (Physiotherapy/Orthotics)

Requirements for Certifcate/Auxiliary Programmes

 

ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

 

WASSCE

SSCE

Age

18 - 35 years

Overall Aggregate

A cutoff aggregate score of Forty Two (42) or better in six subjects, comprising  3 core and 3 elective

A cutoff aggregate score of Twenty Seven (27) or better in six subjects, comprising  3 core and 3 elective

Core Subjects and Minimum Grades

AT LEAST credits (A1-C6)  in three core subjects
i.e English, Mathematics and Integrated Science.

AT LEAST credits (A-D)  in three core subjects
i.e English, Mathematics and Integrated Science.

Elective Subjects and Minimum Grades

AT LEAST passes (A1-E8)  in three Elective subjects

AT LEAST passes (A-E)  in three Elective subjects

 

Basic programmes leading to the award of a certificate/auxiliary are the following:

3. Field Technician (Community Health)
4. Health Assistant Clinical
